Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How do I set a startup password?

    Go to the “Settings” page of the app, find the “Startup Password” option, and follow the prompts to enter and confirm your startup password. This will add an extra layer of security to your app.

  • How do I enable cloud sync?

    Go to the “Settings” page, find the “Cloud Sync” option, and follow the prompts to log into your iCloud account and allow the app to access your iCloud data. Make sure your device is connected to the internet to complete the sync.

  • How is my data encrypted?

    All your password data is locally encrypted using Advanced Encryption Standard (AES). Only you can access this encrypted data, and the app will not transmit your data to other servers or third-party platforms.

  • How do I manage my passwords?

    On the main screen, you can add, edit, and delete password entries. Each entry can include a username, password, website address, and notes, making it easy for you to organize and manage.
